Important Information
This is the first time many of us are doing something like this, and this is our first real-world run of this service. Don't worry, we've tested this a lot, but there are always variables we can't account for. We need y'all to do three things for us:

  • Please do not record the lectures. There is a screenshot below of what that looks like. We don't have extensive storage space on our servers and it could crash the system.
  • Please disable the webcams for your students, but you can leave your webcam on. There's also screenshots below showing you how to do that. This is another server issue because running too many webcams increases CPU usage leading to slow connections and potential for server failure, so please don't do this.
  • If your internet drops while you are teaching a session, do not log out. Click the Retry Now button once you have internet connection and you will be automatically reconnected with your session.
